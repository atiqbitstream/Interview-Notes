### Practice Tips
1. Start with easy problems and gradually increase difficulty
2. Focus on understanding patterns rather than memorizing solutions
3. Practice coding on a whiteboard or paper
4. Time yourself to simulate real interview pressure
5. Review and learn from your mistakes
